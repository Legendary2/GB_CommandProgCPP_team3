"Underline/normal font":

Underlines the selected text fragment, or removes the underline if it was previously.

"Crossed/normal font":

Strikes out the selected text fragment, or removes the strikethrough if it was previously.

"Bold/Normal font":

Makes a piece of text bold, or sets the normal weight if it was bold before.

"Italic/Normal font":

Italicizes a piece of text, or removes italics if italicized before.

"Highlight text":

Change the background color of the selected text fragment.

"Text color":

Change the text color of the selected fragment.
